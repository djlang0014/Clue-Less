Rules/How to play

  1. Login and Lobby:
      2. Create or join a Lobby
  2. Game Setup:
      3. The game host selects the number of players and starts the game.
      Players choose their character tokens from a list of available options.
  3. Virtual Game Board:
      4. Players are presented with a virtual game board.
      They can view the rooms, move their character tokens, and make suggestions/accusations by interacting with the digital interface.
  4. Making Suggestions:
      5. On players' turn, players will move their character token to a room on the virtual board.
      They will be able to select a suspect, a weapon, and a room for their suggestion given.
  5. Disproving Suggestions:
      6. The game system automatically checks the other players' hands for cards that match the suggestion.
      If a player can disprove the suggestion, the system privately notifies them.
  6. Recording Information:
      7. Players maintain an in game notepad or interface to keep track of the information they receive and deduce during the game.
  7. Making Accusations:
      8. Players can make an accusation when they believe they have solved the mystery.
      The system reveals the solution if the accusation is incorrect.
  8. Winning:
      9. The first player to correctly accuse the murderer, weapon, and room wins the game.
      The game system declares the winner and may offer a replay option.
  9. Chat and Interaction:
      10. Players can communicate with each other using in-game chat or messaging features.
      They can strategize, mislead, or discuss the game
  10. End of Game:
      11. After a game concludes, players may choose to start a new one or exit the virtual lobby.
